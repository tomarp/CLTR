CLTR

Chrono-Light Thermal Response is a multimodal controlled warm-exposure study built around a 2 × 2 design crossing bright versus dim light with morning versus midday sessions. It brings together continuous physiological signals, environmental measurements, repeated thermal comfort and sensation ratings, and structured fan-cooling and re-warming phases to support research in thermal comfort, chrono-physiology, and intelligent building control.
