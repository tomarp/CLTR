CLTR Publications

This page presents publications associated with the Chrono-Light Thermal Response project, beginning with the first published paper and the forthcoming data paper.

Published Scientific Reports 2025

Acute effect of light and time of day on thermal physiology, perception, and behavior

Abdelkader Elkounni, Marika Vellei, Jérôme Le Dréau, Marcel Schweiker, Christian Inard.

Scientific Reports, 2025. DOI: 10.1038/s41598-025-22542-w.

This paper reports the first published CLTR findings on how light exposure and time-of-day interact with thermal physiology, perception, and behavioral response.

To Be Submitted Scientific Data Data Paper

Multimodal indoor environmental and physiological recordings during warm exposure with illuminance diurnal timing and airflow cooling

Abdelkader Elkounni, Puneet Tomar, Marika Vellei, Jérôme Le Dréau, Alfonso P. Ramallo-González, Anna Laura Pisello, Christian Inard.

To be submitted to Nature's Scientific Data.

This data paper will present the CLTR multimodal dataset and experimental structure, documenting indoor environmental measurements, physiological recordings, illuminance timing, and airflow cooling protocol design for reuse and reproducible secondary analysis.
